Singer . Actor . Model

Caleb A. Wright is a vocalist and actor hailing from Cleveland Heights, OH. He has been acting since the age of 7 and singing since the age of 16. He was raised ... in a musical environment from an early age and he has been performing with his family's group The Wright Family Singers since birth. He is a classically trained Bass-Baritone and he began classical vocal study at the age of 14. He has been a featured soloists in New York City, Los Angeles, Malibu, Virginia, Washington D.C, Chicago and throughout the state of Ohio. Caleb has been in many shows and he is looking forward to returning to New York City permanently to further his theatrical career in the very near future. Caleb is also a highly trained choral singer and he has been in over 50 choirs and groups since the age of 16. His personal philosophy is "Music is not something to contain, if you have been gifted it is your responsibility to take that gift and change the lives of others." Currently Caleb is an active soloist in the Cleveland Area and he is a member of 21 musical groups, spanning a wide variety of genres. Some of these groups include The Cleveland Orchestra Chorus, The Cleveland Orchestra Chorus Chamber Chorus, and The Voices of Koinonia. Caleb has also been a featured soloists at several local churches including Forest Hill Church Presbyterian, South Euclid United Church of Christ, First Baptist Church of Greater Cleveland, St. Marks Presbyterian Church and Mt. Zion Fellowship of The Brethren. Along with being a wedding singer, Caleb also sings for funerals and corporate events. In 2016 he made his solo debut with The Cleveland Orchestra during the Annual Martin Luther King Day Celebration. Currently in 2017 Caleb is accepting roles, gigs and working on an album project, as well as an album project with The Wright Family Singers being released in summer 2019. Caleb also hopes to be releasing an album with his own band Alexander Wright & The Collective In the next year.
